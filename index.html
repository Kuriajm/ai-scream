About AI-Scream

AI-Scream is a playfully-branded, one-week bootcamp designed to introduce kids aged 8–17 in Kenya to Artificial Intelligence, coding, robotics, and creative problem-solving—all through the joyful lens of ice cream!

Bootcamp Highlights

  • Vanilla Vision – AI & Chatbots
  • Strawberry Sense – Machine Learning
  • Choco-Creativity – AI Art & Games
  • Robo-Ripple – Robotics with Micro:bit
  • Mint Mission – AI for Good Projects
  • Sundae Showcase & Graduation Fudge!

Who It’s For

  • Children ages 8–17
  • Schools and parents seeking engaging tech education
  • Sponsors supporting youth skills development
